Defining Words

Choose File->Definition from the main menu to create a new Definition tab. A Definition tab can be used to show a word's definition and other related information including anagrams, hooks, and extensions. Knowing a word's definition can be useful when studying, as it can make the word and its inflections easier to remember. To see a word's definition, type the word into the input line. Then click the Define button, or press the Enter key. The word will be displayed, along with the word "Acceptable" or "Unacceptable" and the word's definition and related information. If the word is acceptable but has no definition available, the phrase "(no definition)" will be displayed.
